Lazzoni uses cookies and similar technologies to improve the user experience on our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences, track your behavior, and provide personalized content.

Types of Cookies

Lazzoni uses the following types of cookies:
Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly and cannot be disabled. They include cookies that remember your shopping cart, log-in information, and site preferences.
Performance Cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use the website, including the pages you visit, the time you spend on the site, and any errors you encounter. The data is used to improve the website's performance and user experience.
Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and allow the website to provide personalized content and features, such as recommended products and site language.
Advertising Cookies: These cookies collect information about your behavior on websites and other sites, including the products you view and purchase. The data is used to show you relevant advertisements and offers.

Cookie Consent

Using the Lazzoni website, you agree to use cookies by this Cookie Preference. If you do not agree to the use of cookies, you may disable them in your browser settings. However, some website features may not work as intended without cookies.
